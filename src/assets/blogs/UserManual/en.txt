# About

## User Manual

To use the interpreter, simply ENTER your Boolean expresion into the input field and PRESS execute! If you expression is well-formed, you should see a version of your expression the interpreter sees, but if you see nothing, you might want to double check your expression.

You can also play around with the truth value of your expression by changing the truth values of your propositions.
